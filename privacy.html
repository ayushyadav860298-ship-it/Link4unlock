Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

Link4Unlock does not collect any personal information. All links and tasks are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. We do not have access to your data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect any personal information, we don't use or share any data. Your links are stored only on your device.

3. Cookies

Link4Unlock uses localStorage to save your generated links. This data never leaves your browser and is not shared with any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, trackers, or advertising networks. Your privacy is our priority.

5. Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device. We recommend clearing your browser data if you want to remove saved links.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
